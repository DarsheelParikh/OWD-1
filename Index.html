Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication amoung chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensive tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • New born pandas are pink, and not white!
  • Pandas have a 12 hour eating routine
  • Adult Giant Pandas can weigh 45 kilos or 100 pounds!
